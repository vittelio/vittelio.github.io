Vittelio measures how closely your usual eating aligns with the MIND diet, then suggests a few simple swaps — within the meals you already have — to move a little closer to that eating pattern.

Then the whole thing is yours.

Start with your real week

Search the dishes you already eat and roughly how often. A few minutes, once — there's no daily food diary and nothing to log every day.

A real MIND score

Vittelio scores your whole week from 0 to 14 against the published MIND-diet research — an honest read on the pattern you eat, not single foods.

Simple, same-meal swaps

A handful of improvements that fit the meals you already have, ranked best-first and chosen to keep calories in check.

Your own pace

Gentle, Steady, or Full — from your biggest couple of wins to the complete ideal week. Same guidance for everyone; you choose how far.

A recipe for everything

Every recommendation comes with a clear, step-by-step recipe — there's never a suggested dish you don't know how to cook.

A weekly shopping list

Your plan turns into an aisle-by-aisle list, sized to how often you actually cook each dish, so you buy just enough.

Progress over time

Meals log themselves as you cook, and your score moves day by day — gentle and honest, with no streaks to keep or pressure to perform.

Respects what you avoid

Tell it what you skip — nuts, fish, dairy, gluten, vegetarian, or vegan — and every suggestion works around it.

Private by design

No account, no ads, no trackers. Your data stays on your device — Vittelio has no backend server of its own.
