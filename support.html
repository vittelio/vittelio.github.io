Common questions

How do I reset or clear my data?

You can clear your information from within the app, or delete the app from your device — that permanently removes everything stored locally.

Do I need an account?

No. Vittelio requires no account, email, or login, and works fully offline.

Is my information private?

Yes. Your meals, the foods you avoid, your score, and plan stay on your device and are never sent to us. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Is Vittelio medical advice?

No. Vittelio is an educational tool based largely on published research on lifestyle, diet, and brain health and is not a substitute for advice from a qualified healthcare professional.
